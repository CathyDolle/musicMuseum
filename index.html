Welcome
to our lofi museum

Welcome to our little museum ! Its purpose is to offer a relaxing musical experience around “lofi” music. Interact with the instruments to smoothen your ears.

The
concept

We hope you enjoyed your visit ! This website was built in 4 days in the context of a school project. All the elements you can see, except the instruments, were made in Three.js.
